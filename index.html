My top 3 artists & their albums

Rex Orange County
Bcos U Will Never B Free

Raw and introspective, this debut album mixes jazz, indie, and hip-hop influences, showing Rex’s early style and heartfelt lyrics about love and self-discovery.

Apricot Princess

A warm, romantic album filled with lush sounds and more polished production, where Rex reflects on relationships, happiness, and vulnerability.

WHO CARES?

Lighthearted and upbeat, this album combines jazz and pop, focusing on themes of acceptance, self-worth, and moving forward.

Pony

More introspective and somber, "Pony" deals with personal struggles, mental health, and navigating life’s ups and downs, all with Rex's signature mix of pop and soulful melodies.

Frank Ocean
Nostalgia, ULTRA

Frank’s debut mixtape blends R&B with indie sounds, featuring raw storytelling about love and identity, which quickly gained him recognition.

Channel ORANGE

His first studio album, praised for its depth, explores love and social issues with a mix of soul, funk, and jazz, cementing his status in R&B.

Blonde

This experimental album features minimal production and explores themes of love, self-discovery, and introspection, showcasing Frank’s innovative style.

Chanel

A standalone single that delves into duality and identity, blending catchy melodies with introspective lyrics and clever wordplay.

Tyler, The Creator
Wolf

Tyler’s third album blends hip-hop, jazz, and R&B, with complex production and themes of love, personal growth, and self-discovery, showcasing his evolving artistic vision.

Flower Boy

A lush, introspective album that marked a shift in Tyler's style, exploring themes of love, identity, and vulnerability with emotional depth and intricate production.

Igor

An experimental album that tells a story of heartbreak and self-transformation, with soulful, electronic, and rap influences, earning Tyler widespread critical acclaim.

Call Me If You Get Lost

A dynamic mix of rap, jazz, and soul, this album features a travel-themed narrative with Tyler's signature creative production and confident, introspective lyricism.
